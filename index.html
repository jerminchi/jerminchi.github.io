Local Shopper

Local Shopper is a one stop shop for all your local grocery stores. It lets consumers easily choose items they would like, and place an order. As a business user, you’d be able to create your store, add items, and let a consumer know when their order is ready for pickup, and / or delivery.

  • Ionic
  • Angular JS
  • Firebase
